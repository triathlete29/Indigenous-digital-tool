Discovering a Digital Video Teaching Tool from an Indigenous Lens

We welcome you in the spirit of the seven grandfather teachings and hope you enjoy our research website.

Love, Respect, Truth, Honesty, Bravery, Wisdom, and Humility. If you want to learn more about the seven grandfather teachings, you can click here

We acknowledge that we are situated on the sacred lands of the Anishinaabeg at Bawating, specifically the Anishinaabeg from both the Garden River, and Batchewana First Nations. We also acknowledge the Métis people who live in the community. We’d like to share the components of the research project as you navigate the website.

Dr. Rose Cameron, Dr. Simon Xu and Furkan Zengin are exploring an innovative research topic entitled, Designing a Digital Video Teaching Tool from Indigenous Lens. As researchers, we thought it was important to begin exploring how current digital teaching technology could be re-envisioned from an Indigenous lens. The primary purpose of the tool will be to provide a user-friendly platform where teachers and students will be able to collaborate with each other in an Indigenous way. We are familiar with the educational apps that are used in the classroom setting today and we would like to encourage you to help us to re-think, and to re-image their useability and capabilities from an Indigenous perspective.

In this phase of the research, we are collecting data with the aim of conducting a more thorough analysis. There are two main processes for data collection. You may choose to participate in a Face-to-Face Interview or an Online Survey. This information is addressed in the consent form for you. Once you agree to participate in the research project then you can select the best instrument moving forward. We will be audiotaping the Face-to-Face Interviews in order to ensure accuracy of verbatim responses.

After you complete the survey or interview, you will receive a $5 giftcard via your email address. We would like to express our gratitude for your contribution to the project. Your efforts have been greatly appreciated and we are thankful for your involvement in this important initiative.
